Common questions

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

PepTra Pro is billed through your Apple ID. On your iPhone, open Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions (or App Store → your profile → Subscriptions), select PepTra AI, and change or cancel. Cancel at least 24 hours before the renewal date to avoid the next charge.

How do I restore a purchase on a new device?

Sign in with the same Apple ID, open the subscription screen, and tap Restore Purchases. Your access is tied to your account and follows you across devices.

How do I delete my data or my account?

In the app, go to Profile → Privacy / Account. You can erase all your data, or delete your account entirely — both remove your data from our servers. This can't be undone.

Does Meal Scan need my own API key?

Yes. Meal Scan is bring-your-own-key: you add your own AI provider key in the app, and meal photos go straight from your phone to the provider you choose — never through our servers. The rest of the app works without it.

Is PepTra AI medical advice?

No. PepTra AI is an education and personal-tracking tool only. It is not medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, or dosing instruction, and it does not sell or supply any substance. Always consult a licensed healthcare professional before using any peptide.
