Privacy Policy
Effective July 5, 2026

PepTrack is built to be private by default. The short version: your data stays on your phone.

1What we collect

Nothing. PepTrack has no accounts, no sign-up, no analytics, no tracking SDKs, and no server. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal data.

2What stays on your device

Everything you enter is stored locally on your phone, including:

  • Doses, compounds, schedules and injection sites
  • Body profile (age, gender, height, weight, goal weight, photo, name)
  • Nutrition, meals, hydration, sleep and workout logs
  • Side-effect notes and progress photos
  • Settings and preferences

We have no way to see or recover this data. If you delete the app or use Profile → Delete Account, it is gone permanently.

3Optional features that leave your device

These only activate if you turn them on, and only send what is described:

  • Meal Scan: sends the meal photo you pick directly to the AI provider you configure (e.g. Anthropic), using your own API key. The photo and response pass only between your phone and that provider, under their privacy policy. Your key is stored only on your device.
  • Apple Health: with your permission, the app reads steps, calories, sleep, weight and workouts to fill in your logs. Nothing is written back or sent anywhere. Manage permissions in the Health app.
  • Support email: when you tap “Report a Problem” or “Contact Support”, your mail app opens with a draft that includes the app version, an anonymous install ID (random, generated on your phone, linked to nothing) and your device model. You see the full email before deciding to send it.
  • Export Report: generates a PDF on your device that you may choose to share. Sharing is entirely under your control.

4Notifications

Reminders are local notifications, scheduled and delivered entirely on your device. No push server is involved.
